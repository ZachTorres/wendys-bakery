About Me

Hi, I'm Wendy! For as long as I can remember, baking has been my passion and my happy place. There's something magical about transforming simple ingredients into something that brings joy to others.

What started as baking treats for family and friends has blossomed into Wendy's Whisk & Wonder. Every recipe is crafted with care, using quality ingredients and time-tested techniques passed down through generations.

I believe that the best baked goods are made with love, patience, and attention to detail. Whether it's a batch of cookies, a special celebration cake, or fresh bread for your table, I pour my heart into every creation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

For custom cakes, I recommend ordering at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons (spring/summer weddings, holidays). However, I can sometimes accommodate rush orders - just reach out and I'll do my best to help!

Yes! I offer both vegan and gluten-free versions of most items. These require 48 hours notice. I'm also happy to accommodate other dietary restrictions - just let me know what you need!

For regular items, you can cancel anytime before I start baking. For custom orders with a deposit, you can get a full refund if you cancel at least 7 days before your pickup date. Within 7 days, the deposit is non-refundable as I've already purchased ingredients.

Yes! I offer free delivery for orders over $50 within a 10-mile radius. For orders under $50, there's a $5 delivery fee. For areas beyond 10 miles, please contact me for a custom delivery quote.
